Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the resources?

+

Simply sign up for a free account, browse through our collection of notes and videos, and click on any resource to view or download it. All resources are hosted securely on Cloudinary.

Are the resources free?

+

Yes! All learning resources on DevLearn are completely free. Our mission is to make web development education accessible to everyone.

Can I upload my own resources?

+

Currently, only admin users can upload resources to maintain quality standards. If you'd like to contribute, please contact us through our social channels.

What topics are covered?

+

We cover a wide range of web development topics including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, databases, APIs, and more. New resources are added regularly.

How often are new resources added?

+

We add new resources weekly. Follow us on social media or check back regularly to stay updated with the latest tutorials and notes.
